Dijkstra at the blackboard during a conference at ETH Zurich in 1994. He once remarked, "A picture may be worth a thousand words, a formula is worth a thousand pictures."

Here's a short bibliography of Dijkstra :

  • Studied physics at the University of Leiden
  • 1970s - Worked as a research fellow for Burroughs Corporation
  • Worked at the Eindhoven University of Technology in the Netherlands
  • Held the Schlumberger Centennial Chair in Computer Sciences at the University of Texas at Austin
  • Retired in 2000
  • Died August 6, 2002
